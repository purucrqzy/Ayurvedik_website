Shreeyash Ayurvedic College & Hospital Research Center, Aurangabad

Shreeyash Ayurvedic College & Hospital Research Center, Satara Tanda, Aurangbad. Shreeyash Ayurvedic College & Hospital Research Center was established in 2020. The 100 bedded hospital compromises of a well-equipped Panchakarma centre, Operation Theater, well stocked dispensing unit and state-of-the-art hospital and college building and 2500 sq. mts. of botanical garden which holds more than 200 species of medicinal plants,. The hospital is also equipped with X-Ray unit and a high-tech pathology laboratory. We have already bagged No Objection Certificate from the Department of AYUSH, Govt. of India and we are looking forward to be awarded with Letter of Permission for Ayurved College with an intake capacity of 60 for Under Graduate program prescribed by Dept. of AYUSH, Govt. of India

Course We Offer

We are offer many cources in various sectors for students of Institute

Ayurvedic Medicine and Rasayana

Introduction to Ayurveda principles and philosophy, Study of Ayurvedic anatomy, physiology, and pathology, Herbal medicine preparation and application, Diagnosis techniques such as pulse reading and tongue examination.

Ayurvedic Nutrition and
Dietetics

Understanding the principles of Ayurvedic nutrition based on body constitution (Dosha), Analysis of food properties and their effects on health according to Ayurveda, Cooking techniques for preparing balanced Ayurvedic meals.

Ayurvedic Herbology and Botany

Study of medicinal plants used in Ayurveda, Botanical identification and classification of herbs, Extraction methods for obtaining herbal remedies, Pharmacological properties and actions of Ayurvedic herbs.

Ayurvedic Pharmacology and Formulation

Study of Ayurvedic pharmacological principles, Understanding the properties and actions of Ayurvedic herbs and minerals, Methods of preparation and formulation of Ayurvedic medicines.

Ayurvedic Clinical Diagnosis and Case Studies

In-depth study of Ayurvedic diagnostic methods and assessment tools, Analysis of case studies to understand practical applications of Ayurvedic principles, Interpretation of clinical signs and symptoms according to Dosha imbalances.

Ayurvedic Psychology and Mind-Body Medicine

Exploration of the mind-body connection in Ayurveda, Understanding psychological aspects through the lens of Doshas, Techniques for balancing the mind using Ayurvedic principles.
